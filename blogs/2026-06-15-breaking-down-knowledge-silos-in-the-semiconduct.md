Vectara examines how semiconductor engineering knowledge is hard to reach because the data engineers need is multimodal and often on-premise: images, tables, waveforms, characterization plots, OCR, schematics, and text scattered across Jira, Confluence, and SharePoint. The post draws on interviews with semiconductor engineers.
